slim-webp-enc — side-by-side comparison

slim-webp-enc is a pure-TypeScript lossy WebP encoder with semi-lossy alpha for browsers without native WebP encoding (WebKit/Safari). It isn't a full libwebp replacement — no animation, metadata, or general-purpose lossless — but for typical images it encodes in milliseconds at sizes and quality comparable to the native encoders. Each row encodes the same image with slim-webp-enc, the browser's native canvas.toBlob('image/webp') (absent on WebKit), and PNG as a baseline. All previews are the browser's own decode of the encoded bytes.
